Convergence Cloud

Convergence Cloud (CC) is Enterprise communications as a service centric model that navigates and assist clients with most appropriate for their enterprise.

By giving a glance on it one will wonder “If it works why should I care?”

Enterprise Communications will have a phone system, video conferencing and instant or chat messaging tool with different hardware and software platforms.

Convergence Cloud is the solution that provide the Enterprise communications in a Single Platform which uses L2TP, IPSec and SIP trunking that empowers use cases of Voice, Video and Data Communication in an Enterprise cloud.

Convergence Cloud can have flexible cloud deployment models.

Convergence Cloud solutions can provide private, public and hybrid cloud with sharing benefits.

  • Self-service,
  • Location Services,
  • Rapid Provisioning,
  • Flexibility,
  • Agility and Elasticity.

CC gives enterprises to automate and optimize vCollaboration, vTelepresence sessions with continued functional upgrades SIP proxy servers from SMB to Mid-Enterprise to Mega Clouds.

SAI offers two specific Convergence Cloud products:

  • Virtual Telepresence
  • Virtual Collaboration

Virtual Telepresence enables low latency, high performance applications.

Virtual Collaboration enables SDN use case for collaborative Video distribution within the hospitals and other virtual video analytics applications.
