Back-Up & Disaster Recovery

Data Deduplication

SAI will review, design and build a reliable, efficient and cost-effective data protection architecture. This provides restoration and management of select file versions as they are needed.  Utilize today's latest deduplication solutions to reduce backup costs and simplify data recovery.

Multi-Tier Archive Solutions

SAI offers best-in-class archival solutions from the leading vendors. We have several data storage packages that can handle all your needs, whether for one application, such as email, or multiple applications for your corporate archival strategy.

Disaster Recovery

SAI’s backup and recovery solutions simplify the process of protecting your data and your company. Powerful search and discovery tools are available for fast and economical retrieval of critical information.  Lower your business risks and overhead costs while increasing efficiency.

Remote Site Backup

SAI offers several solutions to meet your remote site backup needs, no matter what the size. These sites typically lack the backup infrastructure and staff to support the data protection processes in the central sites. In addition, the cost and availability of high-speed wide area networks prohibit remote sites from leveraging centralized backup infrastructure. We have the tools and resources required to help you design, architect and implement your remote site protection strategy. 

Discovery, Assessment & Design Services

SAI’s storage experts analyze your existing storage architecture, contrast it to your business needs and create the best in class architecture. We will work with your organization to develop a phased implementation and migration plan that will efficiently and cost-effectively allow you to meet your storage goals.

Virtualization

SAI helps your organization select and implement virtualization strategy. Storage virtualization and consolidation allow your staff to more efficiently manage storage resources as a pool. Virtualization is very prevalent in the entire IT organization, starting with servers and now including storage. Virtual Tape Libraries and Virtual Disk subsystems are becoming the standard that will add tremendous value and benefits to your organization.

OpenStack

SAI OpenStack lets users deploy virtual machines and other instances that handle Object and Block storage tasks for managing a cloud environment on the fly. It makes horizontal scaling easy, which means that tasks that benefit from running concurrently can easily serve more or fewer users on the fly by just spinning up more instances.
