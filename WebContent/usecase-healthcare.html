Care – Store- Protect

The Challenge:

Telemedicine today is a growing demand but the challenge of protecting and storing video data is always over looked.

Telemedicine providers are missing a critical step to protect both the hospital and patient when not complying, storing and protecting each video session.

The Solution:

CS - Storage protects the entire video process from end-to-end. Ensuring your data is completely secure beyond what HIPPA requires.

We secure and manage the complete lifecycle of your storage so you no longer have to manage multiple vendors for video, security and storage. SAI Technology does it all under one solution.
